Game rules:

  1. Roll the dice for the highest number, or any other fair method. Each player takes turns rolling the two dice to accumulate points.
  2. Add the numbers rolled on the dice to your current score
  3. If you roll a 1 on either die or both dice, you lose all the points you've accumulated during that turn, and your turn ends. Your score remains the same as it was before the turn.
  4. If you do not roll a 1 on either die, you can choose to roll again (go to step a) or end your turn and keep your accumulated points.
  5. To end your turn and secure your points, you can choose to "Hold." The "Hold" button is a virtual button or agreement between players. When you decide to "Hold," your current turn ends, and your total points for that turn are added to your overall score.
  6. The game continues until one player reaches or surpasses 100 points at the end of their turn. That player is declared the winner, and the game ends immediately.
